Data Deletion Request

At Nisr Market, we respect your privacy. If you signed up using Facebook Login, you can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

You may delete your account and data in the following ways:

  • In-App: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and confirm.
  • By Email: Send an email to nisrmarket@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Account”. We will process your request within 7 days.

What Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your profile information (name, photo, email, phone number)
  • Any activity history or preferences linked to your account
  • Seller information (if applicable)

Need Help?

If you have any questions about account deletion or privacy, please contact us at nisrmarket@gmail.com.
